    <b> ETAB Benchmark Suite and Model Zoo </b>
</h1>

Our benchmark setup is designed to evaluate general task adaptation approaches. Our notion of "task adaptation" is very generalâ€”it includes full representation learning pipelines (i.e., a selection of a model architecture, pre-training task and transfer learning algorithm), or a readily available pre-trained feature representation that is adapted to an echocardiographic task by attaching a task-specific head. This broad notion encapsulates virtually all pre-training approaches, including self-supervised, semi-supervised and supervised learning.
